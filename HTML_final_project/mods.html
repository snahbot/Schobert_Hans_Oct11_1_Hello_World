""...with a little help from my friends..."

With the impressive and flexible technology of the Pyrovision shader and the limited avaiblability of the maps offically supported by the technology, it was only natural that a few people would step up to expand the map list that supports Pyroland. "Improved Pyrovision Support," and "The Pyroland Patchup," are two such examples, taking very different routes in their objective, "IPS," simply focuses on creating templates and replacement.vmts to make Pyrovision as is aviable for as many maps as possible. While "TPP," tries to expand and improve the vision of Pyroland with custom textures and fixes to existing Pyroland implimantation, resulting in fewer additional maps supported. Both are very compleling projects that really display the love and care modders will put into their favorate games if they are given the tools to do so.

In contrast The Pyroland Patchup, also known simply as "Pyroland Overhaul," includes texture fixes and improved assets, such as more fully realized pyroland based teams for RED and Blu. RED is rebranded as "CANDY" With a pepermint logo, and BLU becomes "CHCLT" with their logo becoming a partially unwrapped chocolate bar. In the offical depiction of pyrovision, mentions of either team on wall decals is completely removed. Military stragety maps are also redrawn to show lolipops. More signs have their text replaced with Pyro-esce gibberish, many new textures were created for common props, such as trees and trains engines.
