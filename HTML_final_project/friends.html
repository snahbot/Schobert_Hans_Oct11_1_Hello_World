What's "paradise" without someone to share it with?

A fun and interesting minor feature of Pyroland is an improved sense of compaionship with some of the player's cosmetic items. Primarily in regards to wearable animals, when ever the player kills another without any assistance the kill feed will contribute assistance credit to one of the listed cosmetics below. This feature even respects custom names that might be applied to the compaion consmetic. This feature is purpley cosmetic and does not effect the player's score in any way, or count as an assist if the compaion happens to be "strange" quality.
(You'll just have to take my word that everything I just said makes sense in context.)

Cosmetics with an asterisk (*) are given kill credit instead of the player during solo kills, the player is given kill credit again if assisted by another player.
